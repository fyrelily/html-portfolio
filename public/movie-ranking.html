The Best Movies According to Katie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Smokey and the Bandit

Irreverant 1970s movie with music by Jerry Reed.

Under Paris

A creative shark creature-feature that brings attention to the destruction of the planet and the blindness money causes. Additionally, it shows that humans will animal who adapt to survive to maintain their superiority.

Dancing with the Birds

A visually stunning documentary about Birds of Paradise and their mating habits. Stephen Fry and the excellently selected music raise the experience to a new level. A great movie to watch with your cat.
